This famed ring greatly improves its wearer's health

the ring of Vitality
Regeneration ego
HP+15

Desirability

The ring of Vitality is quite useful, as it has an excellent base type (regeneration) and an excellent, otherwise unobtainable bonus property (extra HP). Most characters will want to wear this if they find it.

History

The ring of Vitality was replaced with the amulet of Vitality in 0.16.

The ring of Vitality was added in 0.10.
