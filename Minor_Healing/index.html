Minor Healing

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.25: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.
Heals a small amount of damage to the caster's body.

For Lesser Healing, the divine player ability with a similar name, see Elyvilon's page.

Minor Healing is a monsters-only spell which restores a moderate amount of health to the caster. The caster's hit dice affects the effectiveness of the spell.

The following enemies cast Minor Healing:

  • A Angel (2d6+3 damage)
  • T Deep troll shaman (2d6+3 damage)
  • R Dryad (2d4+3 damage)
  • g Gnoll shaman (2d1+3 damage)
  • p Ironbound preserver (2d7+3 damage)
  • A Mennas (2d9+3 damage)
  • o Orc high priest (2d5+3 damage)
  • H Sphinx (2d8+3 damage)
  • i Spriggan druid (2d5+3 damage)
  • C Titan (2d10+3 damage)
