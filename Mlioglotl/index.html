A demon prince once possessed a hunger for power that was exceptional even by the standards of his kind. He betrayed his realm to Lugonu, and his reward was to become this twisted mass of corruption, now known only as Mlioglotl.

Useful Info

Mlioglotl, the Unformed Horror is a thrashing horror of Lugonu that can corrupt the floor around him.

Mlioglotl can appear in the following locations:

  • The Swamp
  • The Shoals
  • The Snake Pit
  • The Spider's Nest

Spells

Spell set I
Slot1 Might Natural flag
Slot2 Cause Fear Priest flag
Slot3 Corrupt Priest flag

Tips & Tricks

Followers of Yredelemnul can convert him into a powerful spectral ally via the Bind Soul ability. Mlioglotl's abyssal summons include liches and starcursed masses, which makes him strong enough for the late game.

History

Mlioglotl was added in 0.28.
