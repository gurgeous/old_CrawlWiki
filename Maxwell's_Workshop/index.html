Summary

Maxwell's Workshop is filled with constructs and big workers, from the fire giants and frost giants to angels. Most of the non-constructs here wield branded great maces.

Many of the random artefacts have "dangerous" properties, like rampaging, ponderousness, and rings of fire and ice. There is also a chance for both of Maxwell's inventions - Maxwell's patent armour and Maxwell's thermic engine - to generate here.
