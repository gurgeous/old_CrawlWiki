Summon Swarm is a level 5 Summonings spell which summons a swarm of 1 - 8 friendly monsters. Spell power augments the number of monsters summoned and their duration, but has no impact on the composition of the swarm. Since this is a rod-based spell, spell power is based on your Evocations skill, not Summonings.

Possible summons include:

  • B Worker ant (25%)
  • y Killer bee (25%)
  • s Spider (16.7%)
  • s Scorpion (8%)
  • w Worm (8%)
  • y Vampire mosquito (8%*)
  • y Wasp (8%)
  • b Butterfly (8%)

[*] Worshipers of good gods will never summon vampire mosquitos.

History

In 0.18, rod of the swarm was removed and so was Summon Swarm

Summon Swarm could summon goliath beetles prior to their removal in 0.17.

Summon Swarm used to be capable of summoning wolf spiders as well. When wolf spiders were improved in 0.11, this spell was nerfed to only be capable of summoning regular spiders.
