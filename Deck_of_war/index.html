Name deck of war

A deck of magical cards which are useful before and during battle.

The deck of war selects its cards from the archetype decks across versions according to the following table, selecting each deck with equal probability. Since archetype decks contain varying numbers of cards, the probabilities for individual cards in the physical deck are not all equal.
