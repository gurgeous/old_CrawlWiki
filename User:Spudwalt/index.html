A member of a strain of hairy, cave-dwelling apes. He often spends long hours lying motionless in his den, staring at a glowing panel instead of doing something productive like questing for a so-called "jahb". He rarely emerges, apart from short food-gathering expeditions.

Spudwalt is fairly new to the ranks of the archivists. He rarely follows a set pattern in his contributions, instead moving erratically from one task to another much like a very hirsute bat. He is gradually getting the hang of Dungeon Crawl and can usually make it to at least the Lair branches before getting killed by being stupid.

You hear the distant roaring of an enraged eggplant.

Heyo, spudwalt here. I'm back on the new site! And now I'm an admin.

I've been playing since Version 0.10, so I've been at this about as long as possible without ever doing things like victory dancing or playing a mountain dwarf. I've managed to ascend eight times, one of which was with all 15 runes.

See you all around!
