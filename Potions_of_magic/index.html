Type Potion
Name Potion of Magic
Icon
A valuable potion which grants a person with an infusion of magical energy.

Quaffing a potion of magic restores between 10 to 37 magic points, strongly biased towards the middle values. This potion is rare and immensely useful for spellcasters.

The formula for gained MP is: 10 + (1d28 + 2d29 - 3) / 3

History

  • Prior to 0.5, the potion restored all magic points and had one in three chance of increasing max MP by one.
