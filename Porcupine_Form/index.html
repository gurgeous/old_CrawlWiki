Porcupine Form

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Obsolete: This article refers to an aspect of the game which has been removed. It is retained for historical reference only.


Porcupine form is a bad form which has the following properties:

  • You can't use weapons, armour or jewellery
  • You can't cast spells
  • UC damage: 0 + UC/5 (like Bat Form)
  • Movement speed is 12.5
  • Stealth modifier is 12 (same as minotaurs)
  • Tiny size (good evasion)
  • -3 strength
  • You are spiny (like three levels of the spiny mutation)
  • Immune to constriction
  • You can't bleed

History

Porcupine form was removed in 0.20.

Prior to 0.14, players in porcupine form couldn't butcher corpses.

Porcupine form was added in 0.12.
