  • T:4094: fell through shaft to D:8. Going back up now...
  • T:4197: on D:6, found Blork the orc with a wand. Took two hits, couldn't have taken a third, escaped up stairs. Ran into orc horde, ran up stairs again, back to the stairs I went to D:5 via the second time.
  • T:4549: After another orc horde battle, decided to switch to plate mail instead of the +2 MR robe. My vamp short sword is +3,+1.
  • T:4729: Quaff-ID'd a potion of mutation. got:
    • Protected by bone plates (AC +2, Dex -1)
    • Flexible-but-weak muscles (Dex +1, Str -1)
    • Partially covered in metallic scales (AC +3, Dex -2)
    • Total: AC +5, Dex -2, Str -1, I'll take it!
  • T:4783: Found an armor shop with two randarts! Particularly interesting is the +1 plate mail of the Amoeba (rN+ EV+2 Str+3). Need 950 gold, though, and have... 33. I'll come back later.
  • T:4857: Have 6 of a scroll, so I try it, nothing happens. Probably remove curse then; I'll inscribe it.
  • Done exploring.

D:6

  • Found stairs that didn't leave me back near blork. Did find an imp, and a couple wands that look interesting.
  • T:5374: Found a dwarven cloak of darkness. Neato!
  • T:5870: Killed Blork with wands.
  • T:6034: Went downstairs.

D:7 and 8

  • Found an armor shop on D:7. Forget what else happened.
    • Said armor shop had a couple randarts, one good, but I couldn't afford anything.
  • On D:8, found the entrance to the orcish mines; ran back up.

D:9

  • Found a centaur behind a bush; took it out with wand of fireball, then wand of frosted away the cloud of flame.
  • took out imp, hungry ghost, and phantom, and found stairs to Lair; did not go down.
  • Found stairs to Lair
  • Finished exploring level, taking out stuff like a hippogriff in the process
  • went to lair

Lair

  • Lured up some monster, killed it
  • Went down, got surrounded by rats and a frog, went back up, read scroll of immolation killing all the rats, then killed the frog
  • Saved on D:9, restored, rested, went back down
  • Surrounded again, went up, killed them one at a time
  • T:9337: went back down, lured up some blink frogs
  • T:9382: used trog's hand, used brothers in arms, died.
  • Final score: 3270, new personal best by far!

Lessons learned

  • Blink frogs are pretty buff
  • Activate trog's hand and brothers in arms *before* entering lair, then berserk once you're there
  • Turn off individual-weapons skills so as to train only fighting skill, since you don't know early on what weapons you'll be using the most

  • Always eat immediately if hungry and fighting something nasty, so that you can then berserk. Don't wait until you're one hit from death, then eat.
  • rings of regen are awesome
  • Don't be afraid to try out scrolls when surroudned, you might get immolation or fear.
