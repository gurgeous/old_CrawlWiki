 Book of Flames
Primary school Fire
The standard text used in arcane academies by students of the elemental magic of Fire. Readers are recommended to wear a pair of insulated gloves.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Foxfire Conjuration/Fire 1
b - Conjure Flame Conjuration/Fire 3
c - Inner Flame Fire/Hexes 3
d - Flame Wave Conjuration/Fire 4


Further Reading

The Book of Fire has more advanced Fire Magic.

History

  • 0.28 Added Flame Wave.
  • 0.27 Sticky Flame and Fireball were removed from the book. Prior to this version, this was the starting book for Fire Elementalists. For all classes with magic, starting books were replaced by sets of spells.
  • 0.25 Added Foxfire. Removed Flame Tongue and Throw Flame.
  • 0.10 Conjure Flame was added back, and Bolt of Magma was removed.
  • 0.9 Inner Flame replaced Conjure Flame.
