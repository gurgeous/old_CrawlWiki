A kobold who is in league with demonic powers and can call forth beings from the depths of Hell.

“The Parts Septentrionall are with these Sp’ryts Much haunted.. About the places where they dig for Oare. The Greekes and Germans call them Cobali.”
-Thomas Heywood, _The Hierarchy of the Blessed Angels_, Book IX, l. 568. 1635.

Useful Info

Apprentice kobold demonologists are kobolds that have learned how to summon tier-5 demons. Fortunately, they aren't much more durable than their non-magical brethren. They are exceptionally rare outside of certain sewer vaults.

Tips & Tricks

  • They are fairly fragile, making it almost always a better idea to try to kill them off rather than wasting attacks against their summoned allies. Once the demonologist dies, its summons vanish with it.
