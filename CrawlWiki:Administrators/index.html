An administrator or sysop of CrawlWiki is a user who can:

  • delete and undelete pages
  • protect and unprotect pages from editing
  • edit protected pages, including the site logo and interface
  • block and unblock users from editing

A bureaucrat of CrawlWiki is a user who can also promote other users into administrators or bureaucrats.
