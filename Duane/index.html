Speaks
A mercenary whose only significant trait is his unusually large ears. He was hired to guard something (or possibly someone), but who or what that is, he can't tell you. If he did, he'd have to kill you. In fact, he'd like to just kill you anyway.

Useful Info

Duane was a weak human unique.

Spells: Magic Dart (3d6), Slow, Haste, and Blink.

Tips & Tricks

High magic resistance or an amulet of resist slowing will protect you from Slow.

History

Duane had his flavor text updated and his experience value cut in 0.6, and was removed in 0.8
