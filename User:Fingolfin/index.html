  • Damn, 12 runes and then Serpent of Hell bundles in and ruins it all :/ Fingolfin the Metallomancer
  • I just don't seem to get the hang of Dis:7 : Fingolfin the Petrodigitator, GrWz of Vehmet
  • FIRST 15-RUNE VICTORY EVER !!!!!! : Fingolfin the Minotaur Barricade, MiFi of Ru
