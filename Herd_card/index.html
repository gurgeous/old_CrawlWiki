Herd card

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Obsolete: This article refers to an aspect of the game which has been removed. It is retained for historical reference only.

The Herd card summons 1d(power/3)+1 animals (minimum 2). Fifty percent of the time will result in only one kind of monster; otherwise it's randomly jumbled. Higher card power reduces the odds of some animals arriving hostile.

Possible summons include:

  • r Orange rat
  • Y Sheep
  • Y Yak
  • h Hog
  • a Soldier ant
  • h Wolf
  • h Polar bear
  • h Black bear
  • l Basilisk
  • l Komodo dragon
  • F Spiny frog
  • h Hound
  • Y Elephant
  • H Hippogriff
  • H Griffon

History

The Herd card could be found in the following decks over the past several versions. Prior to 0.15, the card also could summon a grizzly bear, a monster removed in that version. It was removed in 0.16.
