0.12 Crawl Tournament

The new version of Crawl is nearly here, and with it comes another Crawl tournament (May 11-26). Anybody think they might be interested in signing up for the team? We got 4th place last year, and I'd love to reach the top 3 this time (unlikely though that may be).

Competing basically involves playing the latest version of the game online through any of the primary servers. The majority of the points a team earns are awarded for gathering runes, winning (preferably with combinations that haven't been won yet or multiple times in a row), and killing player ghosts, though you should see the official rules for the full scoring breakdown. The tournament lasts for just over two weeks, during which time you may attempt as many games as you like.
