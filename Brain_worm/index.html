Useful Info

A brain worm is an evil insect with a smiting-type intelligence draining attack, making it a pest to spellcasters and a potential source of stat death to characters with low intelligence scores.

Tips & Tricks

  • A ring of sustain attributes will protect you from the loss of intelligence. Stat zero is a concern, but brain worms are so weak you should be able to kill them long before then. Just be careful if you come across multiple brain worms at once, and prioritise them over other monsters if you're reliant on your intelligence to cast spells or if your intelligence is dangerously low.

History

Brain worms were removed in 0.18
