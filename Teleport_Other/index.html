 Teleport Other
Level 3
School1 Translocation
Source(s) Book of Displacement
There-And-Back Book
Casting noise 3
Spell noise 0
Power Cap 100
Range LOS
Flags Wl check, Dir or target, Escape, Needs tracer, Not self
Randomly translocates a targeted creature.

Teleport Other is a level 3 Translocations spell which causes a monster to randomly teleport a few turns later. High willpower defends against it, and there is no way to control where the target will land. As the name implies, you cannot target yourself with it.

Strategy

Unless you specifically want to make annoying player ghosts, this spell usually isn't worth learning. It's resistible, takes several turns to activate, and, just as a random teleport can land you right back where you started, so too can teleporting a strong monster.

History

  • Prior to 0.28, monsters could cast this spell.
  • Prior to 0.27, the naga mage and nagaraja could cast this spell.
  • Prior to 0.17, hellwings could cast this spell.
  • Prior to 0.12, the spell worked less often.
