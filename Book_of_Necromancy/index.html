 Book of Necromancy
Primary school Necromancy
A macabre book containing basic Necromancy spells. Readers are recommended to wear a pair of gloves they don't mind disposing of afterward.

The Book of Necromancy is the starting spell book for Necromancers.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Pain Necromancy 1
b - Animate Skeleton Necromancy 1
c - Vampiric Draining Necromancy 3


History

  • In 0.22, BVC was removed from this book.
  • In 0.21, Borgnjor's Vile Clutch was added, for testing purposes, replacing Control Undead.
  • In 0.15, Lethal Infusion was removed.
  • In 0.10, Dispel Undead was replaced with Control Undead.
