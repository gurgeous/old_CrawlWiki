Bolt of Magma

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.
Fires a penetrating bolt of molten rock. A portion of its damage bypasses fire resistance.

Bolt of Magma is a monster-only spell which deals respectable fire damage. It has a shorter range than most bolt spells, and loses one square of range each time it hits a target, but only 55% of the damage it deals is affected by fire resistance.

The following enemies cast Bolt of Magma:

  • q Draconian scorcher (3d25 damage)
  • 9 Molten gargoyle (3d15 damage)
  • O Ogre mage (3d18 damage)
  • 8 Roxanne (3d23 damage)
  • N Salamander mystic (3d18 damage)
