Version 0.28: This article is up to date for the latest stable release of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.
Throws a small puff of flame.

Throw Flame is a largely monster-only spell that shoots a puff of flame at a single target in LOS.

The following enemies cast Throw Flame:

  • o Blork the orc (3d5 damage)
  • P Burning bush (3d7 damage)
  • e Dowan (3d5 damage)
  • o Orc wizard (3d5 damage)
  • @ Sigmund (3d5 damage)

Players may duplicate the effect with a wand of flame.
