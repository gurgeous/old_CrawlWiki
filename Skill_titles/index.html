Your character will be given a skill title based on their current skill set. To determine the title, a character's skills are placed into five tiers: Lvl 1-7, 8-14, 15-20, 21-26, and 27. Your character's title is determined by their highest skill -- any time a different skill surpasses your current highest skill, your title will change to reflect it. Your title will also change when your current highest skill reaches a new tier. The first skill to reach level 27 will determine your character's final title.

Custom Titles

Certain species/skill combinations result in custom titles:

  • Barachim + 27 Invocations + No God = "God-Hated"
  • Deep Elf + 27 Bows = "Master Archer"
  • Deep Elf + 27 Short Blades = "Blademaster"
  • Demonspawn + 27 Invocations + Evil god = "Blood Saint"
  • Draconian + 27 Air Magic = "Storm Dragon"
  • Draconian + 27 Earth Magic = "Iron Dragon"
  • Draconian + 27 Fire Magic = "Fire Dragon"
  • Draconian + 27 Hexes = "Faerie Dragon"
  • Draconian + 27 Ice Magic = "Ice Dragon"
  • Draconian + 27 Poison Magic = "Swamp Dragon"
  • Felids + Unarmed Combat = "Scratcher", "Gouger", "Ripper", "Eviscerator", "Sabretooth"
  • Formicid + 27 Translocations = "Teletunneler"
  • Gargoyle + 27 Invocations + Jiyva = "Rockslime"
  • Merfolk + 27 Long Blades = "Swordfish"
  • Mummy + 27 Fire Magic = "Highly Combustible"
  • Mummy + 27 Invocations + Nemelex Xobeh = "Forbidden One"
  • Mummy + 27 Unarmed Combat = "Pharaoh"
  • Ogre + Any level of Spellcasting = "Ogre Mage"
  • Palentonga + 27 Invocations + Good god = "Holy Roller"
  • Palentonga + 27 Invocations + Qazlal = "Rolling Thunder"
  • Palentonga + 27 Polearms = "Prickly Pangolin"
  • Spriggan + 27 Necromancy = "La Petite Mort"
  • Troll + 21 or more Conjurations = "Wallbreaker"
  • Vine Stalker + 27 Invocations + Nemelex Xobeh = "Black Lotus"
  • Vine Stalker + 27 Necromancy = "Corpse Flower"
