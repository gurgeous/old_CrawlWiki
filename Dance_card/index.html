Dance card

The card depicts an extravagantly-dressed, masked figure dancing alone in a vast room.

The Dance card summons a temporary friendly dancing weapon depending on power level.

Power Level 0

  • Weapon: Dagger, Club, or runed Short sword
  • Enchantment: -3 to 0

Power Level 1

  • Weapon: Long sword, Hand axe
  • Brand: Flaming (25%), Freezing (25%)
  • Enchantment: -1 to +2

Power level 2

  • Weapon: Great sword, executioner's axe
  • Brand: Speed (50%), Electrocution (50%)
  • Enchantment: +2 to +5
