 Book of the Sky
Primary school Air
A magical book containing powerful elemental spells of Air. The great minotaur hero Vuxhurn, given a copy of this book while trapped atop the tower of a wizard with a particularly perverse sense of humor, used its pages to construct a rudimentary glider and soared to freedom.

The Book of the Sky has focus on Air Magic. It contains several powerful offensive spells and an enchantment which can be useful to any character.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Summon Lightning Spire Air/Summoning 4
b - Storm Form Air/Transmutation 7
c - Maxwell's Capacitive Coupling Air 8


History

  • In 0.17, Flight was replaced by Summon Lightning Spire.
  • In 0.12, Insulation was removed.
  • In 0.9, Lightning Bolt was replaced by Tornado.
