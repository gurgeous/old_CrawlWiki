Air Magic

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.26: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Air Magic skill determines how easily you can use spells involving air and electricity. Most Air Magic can be resisted with electricity resistance. There are also several wind-resistant enemies. Air Magic partners well with Conjurations.
