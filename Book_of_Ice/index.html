 Book of Ice
Primary school Ice
A book of advanced magical spells using the element of Ice. Readers are recommended to wear a pair of warm gloves.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Freezing Cloud Air/Conjuration/Ice 5
b - Simulacrum Ice/Necromancy 6
c - Polar Vortex Ice 9
