A skilled warrior, looking to further develop his reputation as a great slayer of monsters. Be warned: he defines 'monster' rather more liberally than most dictionaries.

Useful info

Norbert was a unique human warrior you might have encountered in the mid-game. He was similar to Maud in most ways. He was removed in 0.8.

Tips and tricks

Not a challenge, despite what the game would have you think.
