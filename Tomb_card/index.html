Tomb card

The card depicts a royal crypt, its entrance sealed by a heavy stone slab.

The Tomb card surrounds the player with a single layer of temporary rock walls. This layer may not be complete, and the duration is calculated by: 10 + power/20 + 1d(power/4) – 1.

  • Minimum duration (0 evocations, plain deck, without Nemelex): 10 turns.
  • Maximum duration (27 evocations, legendary deck, full piety with Nemelex): 62 + 1d268 turns.

Propagation of effects

The entombment effect iterates and checks each adjacent tile surrounding the player. If the following are found, further effects to that tile do not occur:

  • If a 'one-in-power' chance occurs, or;
  • If the tile contains a monster, any kind of wall, a door, a portal entrance, an altar, deep water, etc.

If the above are not found, the check continues:

  • Any clouds and mechanical traps in the tile are destroyed.
  • The terrain type of the tile is changed to an ordinary rock wall.
  • Any items on the floor will be displaced to the square where you are standing.
