Bolt of Fire

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.
Fires a penetrating bolt of flames.

Bolt of Fire is a monster-only spell capable of striking multiple targets along its path, dealing a considerable amount of fire damage. Fire resistance reduces the damage dealt by this spell by 50% (rF+), 66% (rF++), or 80% (rF+++). The Fire Breath monster ability is very similar to this spell.

The following enemies cast Bolt of Fire:

  • & Asmodeus (3d26 damage)
  • R Azrael (3d20 damage)
  • 2 Balrug (3d23 damage)
  • e Deep elf pyromancer (3d17 damage)
  • q Draconian scorcher (3d25 damage)
  • R Efreet (3d15 damage)
  • C Fire giant (3d25 damage)
  • D Golden dragon (3d27 damage)
  • p Hell knight (3d18 damage)
  • @ Maggie (3d13 damage)
  • R Mara (3d27 damage)
  • @ Margery (3d32 damage)
  • G Ophan (3d24 damage)
  • * Orb of fire (3d40 damage)
  • o Orc sorcerer (3d17 damage)
  • Q Tengu reaver (3d26 damage)

The following enemies may be able to cast Bolt of Fire, depending on their spell set:

  • & Pandemonium lord (5.2% chance)
