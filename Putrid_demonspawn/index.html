A half-mortal, half-demonic creature, with its hellish heritage asserting itself through a certain devilish cast to its features.


It is surrounded by sickly fumes and gases.

Useful Info

Putrid demonspawn are demonspawn with a passive ability similar to the Powered by Death demonspawn mutation. They can be found in the Pandemonium and guarding it's portals in the Depths.

Tips & Tricks

Try to get them in an area without corpses, as nearby corpses will increase their regeneration rate. Try not to use poison against them unless you have access to lethal poison, since they have weak poison resistance. Like all demonspawn, they are also vulnerable to holy weapons and silver, so use those if you have them.
