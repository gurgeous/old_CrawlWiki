Fire Summon

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.22: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Fire Summon is a monster-only spell which is essentially an enhanced version of the effects of the Sceptre of Asmodeus. This spell summons 1-4 of the following fire-related demons, with the following weightings:

R Efreet- (25%)

3 Sun demon- (25%)

2 Balrug- (25%)

2 Hellion- (16.7%)

1 Brimstone fiend- (8.3%)

Unlike demons summoned by the Sceptre, these demons may be abjured.

The following enemies cast Fire Summon:

  • & Asmodeus
