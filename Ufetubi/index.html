Useful Info

Ufetubi are fast, puny tier-5 demons which hound you, dealing minor damage. They can be found in the Abyss or when summoned by other monsters. Ynoxinuls in particular summon hordes of them.

Tips & Tricks

Even in large numbers, ufetubi are hardly a threat. When summoned in droves, they will usually outpace other threats and surround you, bogging you down. While this will slow melee threats from reaching you, smite and hellfire attacks will still blast you regardless (though the latter will kill the ufetubi). Don't waste time killing ufetubi while actual threats are around, unless they're blocking your way to a smiter.
