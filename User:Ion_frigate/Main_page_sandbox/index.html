The Crawl Wiki is mostly back up, although a few images and links may still be broken.
New contributors are always welcome! If you would like to register to for a new account, please send us an email with your preferred username.
If you just want info on DCSS, the old wiki has been put up at old-crawl.chaosforge.org. Username: old_crawl, Password: dungeon.

This is a community-edited encyclopedia that aims to document all the information in the roguelike game Dungeon Crawl. We are currently in the process of updating this site now that version 0.11 has been released.

We need your help to maintain our site. If you find out something interesting, spot an error, or just want to help, please sign up. For those of you who have never added something before, don't worry. It's not very challenging and the wiki has a very useful help section explaining in detail how to do everything. Head over to the Community Portal to see what is going on at the moment, or take a look at some of the currently listed projects.

You may also wish to visit the official homepage of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup, or go directly to the download of the most recent version.

This wiki contains massive spoilers. If you do not wish to have your game experience spoiled, stop reading now!

Play Crawl Online Now
  • CAO - The largest and almost official Crawl server, located in North America, is mostly online.
  • CDO - CAO's European counterpart. Online, hosts 0.9, 0.10 and 0.11.
  • CSZO - Crawl server located in Pennsylvania, in the United States. Online, hosts up to 0.11, as well as trunk and inception.
  • CSN - Crawl server located in Nevada, in the United States. Hosts webtiles trunk version.
  • NEO - Independent Crawl and NetHack server. Hosts up to 0.8.
  • RHF - Crawl and NetHack server that doesn't use DGameLaunch. Hosts up to 0.7.
