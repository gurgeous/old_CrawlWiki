 Book of Frost
Primary school Ice
A book of magic spells, often used as an introduction to the elemental magics of Ice. Its pages feel unusually brittle, as if they'd crumble at a touch.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Freeze Ice 1
b - Frozen Ramparts Ice 3
c - Hailstorm Conjuration/Ice 4


Further reading

The Book of Ice has more advanced ice magic.

History

  • 0.27 Summon Ice Beast and Ozocubu's Armour were removed from the book. Prior to this version, this was the starting book for Ice Elementalists. For all classes with magic, starting books were replaced by sets of spells.
  • 0.25 Throw Frost and Throw Icicle were replaced by Frozen Ramparts and Hailstorm.
  • 0.18 Condensation Shield was removed.
  • 0.9 Freezing Cloud was replaced by Condensation Shield.
