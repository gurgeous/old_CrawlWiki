"How doth the little busy Bee
Improve each shining Hour,
And gather Honey all the day
From every opening Flower!"

-Isaac Watts. 1715.

Useful Info

Bumblebees are larger, slower versions of killer bees. They deal twice as much damage and more poison with their stings, have double the HP, and are worth triple the XP. Fortunately, they're much slower, have less EV, and are still just as susceptible to poison. They can occasionally be found in the Lair, but thankfully not in the huge swarms killer bees tend to appear in.

Tips & Tricks

  • Poison and confusion are very effective against them.

History

Bumblebees were removed in 0.12.

Prior to 0.11, their description consisted merely of one very memorable phrase:

A very large and fat hairy bee.
