Throws a small puff of frost.

Throw Frost is a level 2 Conjurations/Ice Magic monster-only spell which shoots a puff of frost at a single target in LOS.

The following enemies cast Throw Frost:

  • o Blork the orc (3d5 damage)
  • e Dowan (3d5 damage)
  • 5 White imp (3d5 damage)

History

In 0.25, Throw Frost was removed from the list of spells available to players.
