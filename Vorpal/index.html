Vorpal

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.25: This article is up to date for the latest stable release of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.
It inflicts extra damage upon your enemies.

Vorpal is a brand which any weapon may have. Melee weapons with the vorpal brand do extra damage of at least 1 and up to 33% each hit, averaging +16.6%. Ranged weapons do 20% more damage. There is a 15.8% chance to brand a melee weapon and a 19% chance to brand a ranged weapon with vorpal when a scroll of brand weapon is used on a non-artifact weapon. This chance increases if the weapon already has a brand other than vorpal.

Tips & Tricks

  • This brand works best on high-damage weapons, since the damage is multiplicative.
  • Vorpal weapons are inferior damage-wise to most other brands, but versatile because nothing can resist the extra damage.

History

  • Prior to 0.25, different types of weapons used different descriptors based on the type of damage they inflicted ("Piercing", "Chopping", "Slicing", "Crushing", "Velocity", etc.).
  • Prior to 0.16, this brand was called "stabbing" on short blades.
  • Prior to 0.13, this brand increased your damage between 0% and 25% each hit, averaging 12.5%.
