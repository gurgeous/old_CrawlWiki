A military axe with a long haft and a single-bladed head.
This weapon falls into the 'Axes' category. It is a one handed weapon.

This is a step up from the hand axe in terms of power, but you should still try to upgrade to a stronger axe eventually.

Like all axes, this weapon allows the player to cleave.
