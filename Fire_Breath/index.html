Fire Breath

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.21: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Fire Breath is a monster-only spell which bellows out a gout of flame, dealing 3d(HD*2) fire damage to everything in its path (functioning almost exactly like Bolt of Fire). Barring fire resistance, this can be a serious threat.

The following enemies cast Fire Breath:

  • D Fire dragon (3d24 damage)
  • h Hell hound (3d10 damage)
  • Y Hellephant (3d40 damage)
  • k Lindwurm (3d18 damage)
  • Y Nellie (3d40 damage)
  • D Serpent of Hell (Gehenna) (3d34 damage)
