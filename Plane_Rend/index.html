Plane Rend

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article is up to date for the latest stable release of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.
Summons some of the greater threats from one of the Pits of Slime, the Elven Halls, the Vaults, the Crypt, the Tomb of Ancients, or the Realm of Zot; the specific branch varies with each cast of the spell.

Plane Rend is a monster-only spell which summons 1-3 monsters from one of the following randomly selected branches, with each branch being of equal likelihood. Its summon cap is 6.

Branch Monster, chance in branch cast[1]
The Slime Pits J Rockslime 35.71% J Acid blob 35.71% J Titanic slime creature 28.51%
The Elven Halls e Deep elf sorcerer 38.46% e Deep elf high priest 38.46% e Deep elf blademaster 23.08%
The Vaults 9 War gargoyle 45.45% p Vault sentinel 36.36% p Ironbound convoker 18.18%
The Crypt V Vampire knight 35.71% W Flayed ghost 35.71% z Revenant 28.51%
The Tomb H Sphinx 38.46% M Mummy priest 38.46% z Ancient champion 23.08%
The Abyss X Starcursed mass 45.45% t Apocalypse crab 36.36% X Tentacled starspawn 18.18%
The Realm of Zot D Golden dragon 45.45% y Moth of wrath 36.36% q Draconian stormcaller 18.18%

The following enemies cast Plane Rend:

  • 6 Demonspawn corrupter

History

  • The monster set has been modified in 0.15, 0.16, 0.19, and 0.28. It lost Lair and Orc representation in a previous version, then lost Snake, Swamp, Shoals, and Spider representation in 0.28.
  • Plane Rend was added in 0.14.
