Borgnjor's Mausoleum is a wizlab.

Summary

"Split map layout with corporeal undead on one side and incorporeal undead on the other. Lost souls spawn continuously, and the ending includes some halazid warlocks and their servants. Loot is necromancy themed, as you'd expect"
