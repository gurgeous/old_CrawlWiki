Borgnjor's Vile Clutch, also known as BVC and zombie hands is a level 5 Necromancy/Earth spell in the books of Necromancy, Dreams and Unlife. It affects all enemies in a smite-targeted 3x3 area with the constriction status (displayed as "constricted by zombie hands", which deals damage over time until the affected monster(s) die, escape constriction (which they must waste turns to do), or the effect runs out. In most cases, the monster will die or escape before the status runs out. If you leave line of sight of any affected enemies, they are immediately released.

Resistant enemies

Only a small number of monsters are not affected by BVC, all of which are also immune to regular types of constriction. They are as follows:

  • All slimes and jellies.
  • Insubstantial enemies, including wraith-type undead, many monsters in the Abyss, and orbs of fire.
  • Spiny enemies, consisting of porcupines, torturous demonspawn, and Hell Sentinels.

Uses

BVC is a very versatile spell. It can kill a large number of enemies in a few casts, and has a few specific benefits:

  • Cannot be dodged and is largely irresistible (except for creatures listed above).
  • Pins the target in place, allowing the player to move away even from fast enemies.
  • Reduces the target's EV, making them easier to hit with melee or other destructive spells. This combos particularly well with bolt spells, as BVC pins several creatures at once; it also works well with other high-level Earth Magic spells like Iron Shot or Lehudib's Crystal Spear, both of which suffer somewhat from low accuracy.
  • Makes the target waste turns trying to escape, similar to nets. Enemies that can reach you will still attack you in melee, but this means BVC's effect will last longer since they're no longer trying to escape.
  • Destroys groups of enemies, since it affects an area.
  • Can be used to pick off enemies behind other enemies, since it is smite-targeted.

History

  • Prior to 0.22, BVC was also found in the book of Necromancy and it did 33% more damage than currently.
  • The spell was added in 0.21.
