A grotesque parody of nature, this fantastical creature consists of three individual beasts combined into one.

Useful Info

Chimeras are 3-headed hybrid monsters that can only be generated by evoking the box of beasts. They use the primary attacks of the second two monsters as its auxiliary attacks and can use spells and special abilities from all three monsters.

The three parts of a chimera are chosen from this list: Butterfly, Rat, Bat, Jackal, Adder, Hound, Water moccasin, Sky beast, Crocodile, Hog, Ice beast, Yak, Polar bear, Wyvern, Wolf, Alligator, Grizzly bear, Warg, Elephant, Griffon, Black bear, Catoblepas, Death yak, Anaconda, Raven, Dire elephant, Dragon, Ancient bear, Apis, Hellephant and Golden dragon

History

Chimeras were replaced by mutant beasts in 0.17.

Chimeras were added in 0.13.
