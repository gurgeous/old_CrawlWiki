Version 0.29: This article is up to date for the latest stable release of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.
A pair of thick steel gauntlets. They were Okawaru's reward for a mighty warlord. The owner is destined to die in battle, but what a death it will be!

the +3 pair of gauntlets of War
+3 gloves
+5 slaying

Desirability

The gauntlets of War[1] provide a solid chunk of general defense and a +5 slaying bonus in the gloves slot. What more could you want? Very useful for any character, and definitely superior to any non-artefact option available, if only due to the defense.
