A sharp-toothed predatory denizen of the waters, the shark evolved over millions of years into an almost perfect killing machine.


Useful Info

Sharks are aquatic pests which are much less dangerous than you'd expect. They deal slightly more damage than giant goldfish, and trade a little more AC for a little less EV. So long as you face them while standing on the shore, they aren't too much of a threat. They can be found in the Shoals.

Tips & Tricks

  • Ranged attacks or poison will help keep them from fleeing out to sea.

History

Sharks were removed in 0.15.
