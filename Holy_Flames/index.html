Holy Flames

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.25: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.
Traps an enemy in a ring of blessed flame. These flames are especially dangerous to undead and demonic creatures, and have no effect on holy creatures.

Holy Flames is a monster-only spell which fills every tile around the target with short-lived clouds of blessed fire. These can deal very heavy damage to demonic and undead characters if they choose to walk through them, but blinking out is safe. The clouds do no harm to worshippers of good gods or Qazlal, though.

The following enemies cast Holy Flames:

  • G Ophan
