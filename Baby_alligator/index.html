A juvenile alligator. While it does not have crushing jaws, nor the strength to put on massive bursts of speed, it is more limber than the adult form, and its milk-tooth is still very sharp.

“Alligators commit errors of diet.”
-Bennet Bowler, M.D., _Contributions to the Natural History of the
Alligator, (Crocodilus Mississipiensis), with a Microscopic Addendum_,
p. 17. 1846.

Useful Info

Baby alligators are small cold-blooded pests that lurk underwater before rushing you with surprising speed, much like a full grown alligator would. Although they can only deal moderate damage, they may appear in packs, often in the company of a mature alligator. Baby alligators can be found in the Swamp.

Tips & Tricks

  • All alligators move frighteningly fast when travelling through water, and the Swamp provides few opportunities for limiting the number of units you face at once. If you're not a heavily armoured melee fighter, don't be afraid to teleport or blink to safety if you see a pack closing in on you.

History

Baby alligators were removed in 0.17.
