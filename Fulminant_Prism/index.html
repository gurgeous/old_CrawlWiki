Useful Info

Fulminant Prism is a level 4 Conjurations/Hexes spell which creates an explosive prism at a smite-targeted location of your choosing. This prism will detonate with a radius-2 explosion after 20 auts, dealing considerable physical damage that can't be blocked or dodged (but which is reduced by AC as normal). The prism is fairly fragile and can be destroyed by enemy attacks (though it is immune to most elemental damage). If destroyed prematurely, the prism will create either a smaller, weaker explosion, or no explosion at all (if it is destroyed immediately after being created).

Tips & Tricks

  • Although you need to place your prism carefully to keep it from being destroyed by monsters, the smite targeting, large area of effect, decent damage output, and ability to blow things up beyond your line of sight makes it a very useful spell. Although there are more time- and MP-efficient methods for taking out powerful, well-armored opponents, it's excellent for wiping out swarms of smaller foes or taking out statues, oklob plants, and dangerous eyeballs from a position of safety.
  • Don't blow yourself up.

History

  • Prior to 0.14, Fulminant Prism was a level 5 spell.
  • Fulminant Prism was added in 0.12.
