Corona

Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Corona is a status effect and monster-only Hexes spell which causes the target to glow with an aura of light.

Effect

For the duration of the spell, the target will be easier to hit (attackers gain a to-hit bonus of 1d8 + 1). This spell also prevents you from becoming invisible, outlining your silhouette with light. The spell must have line of fire to the target to cast, and it must overcome the target's willpower to take hold.

A couple of player effects still cause Corona, like Ru's Aura of Power and a piece from Xom's chessboard.

The following enemies cast Corona:

  • e Dowan
  • c Faun
  • g Gnoll shaman
