A green-glowing gaseous entity, flickering and pulsing irregularly as it hovers through the air. Travelers have been known to confuse these for distant lanterns - a mistake rarely made twice.

Useful Info

Will-o-the-wisps are intangible puffs of flames found primarily in the Swamp. When casting, they will create two marshlights; like the Foxfire spell, they are entities that will deal damage on contact.
