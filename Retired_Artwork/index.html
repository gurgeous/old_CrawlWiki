Retired Artwork

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Crawl Stone Soup Tiles has not been known for particularly pretty graphics, but its design standards have grown better and better over time. Over the course of its many versions, volunteer artists have designed and redesigned the artwork for all the game's many monsters, items, and environment. Here are all the retired artworks that have at some point or other been removed from the game.
