Useful Info

Eleionomae are tree-hugging nymphs found in the Swamp. They can fire bolts at you, and when adjacent to a tree, can cast Woodweal to heal themselves. When killed, their bones may turn into a random wand (a net 50% chance for this to happen).

Spells

Spell set I
Slot1 Splinterspray (3d24) Magical flag
Slot2 Woodweal Magical flag,
Emergency flag

Tips & Tricks

  • Although Splinterspray can deal up to 72 damage, its damage is lowered by AC three times as much as normal spells.
  • Try not to fight them next to trees - they can heal themselves back up to full when almost dead.

History

  • Prior to 0.28, Eleionmae did not drop wands on death.
  • Prior to 0.27, Eleionmae destroyed trees whenever they used Woodweal.
  • Eleionomae were added in 0.26.
