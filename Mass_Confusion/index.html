“Go to, let us go down, and there confound their language, that they may not understand one another's speech. So the LORD scattered them abroad from thence upon the face of all the earth: and they left off to build the city.”
-KJV Bible, Genesis 11:7-8.

Mass Confusion is a level 6 Hexes spell that tries to confuse all monsters in your line of sight. Unlike Alistair's Intoxication, it works on monsters that are non-natural, poison resistant, or of animal or lower intelligence. However, it is resistible by magic resistance, making it ineffective against many monsters in the later game.

History

Mass Confusion was removed in 0.18.

In 0.10 Mass Confusion received a small power boost.
