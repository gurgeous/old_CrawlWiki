A small flying demon.

Useful Info

Midges were among the most insignificant of demons. With their fondness for blinking, they were somewhat like crimson imps that hit twice as hard, but did not regenerate. They could be found in the Abyss, Pandemonium, and the Hells.

Tips & Tricks

None needed.

History

Midges were removed in 0.11.
