Useful Info

Swamp dragons are medium-sized dragons which breathe poisonous clouds at you, but are otherwise less threatening than most dragons. You can usually find a few of them in the Swamp, particularly in the rune chamber on Swamp:4.

Breath flag,
Noisy flag

Tips & Tricks

  • Poison resistance takes most of the fight out of swamp dragons. If you lack this, you can stand in one of the naturally-occurring clouds of the Swamp to be (temporarily) safe from its breath.
  • Slaying a swamp dragon sometimes produces swamp dragon scales. This provides poison resistance, naturally useful in a poison-themed branch like the Swamp.

History

Prior to 0.19, this monster dropped a hide that could be turned into swamp dragon armour with a scroll of enchant armour.
