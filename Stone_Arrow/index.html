Stone Arrow is a level 3 Earth Magic/Conjurations spell which fires a sharp rock at a single opponent, dealing physical damage. Earth Elementalists start with this spell.

Tips & Tricks

  • Stone Arrow is just an overall reliable spell. It deals fair and irresistable damage all throughout the Dungeon, Lair, and Orcish Mines.
  • It's also a strong spell for Spellforged Servitor. Monster Stone arrow does not have the same power cap, meaning servitors can do much more. However, players with Servitor will likely have the skill for Iron Shot, which is a stronger spell.

Monster Version

The monster version is similar to the player version, except its power cap is much greater.

The following enemies cast Stone Arrow:

  • e Dowan (enraged) (3d8 damage)
  • 9 Gargoyle (monster) (3d12 damage)
  • @ Louise (3d20 damage)
  • i Spriggan druid (3d18 damage)

History

In 0.27, Stone Arrow's damage was increased.
