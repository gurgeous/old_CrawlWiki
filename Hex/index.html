Hexes are spells that affect things and monsters (usually negatively). They mostly ignore dealing direct damage in favour of inflicting debilitating status effects on enemies, but there are also a few spells that enhance your own combat abilities in some way.

History

  • In 0.18, the level 5 spell Yara's Violent Unravelling was added to the Book of Hinderance and it was renamed to Book of Misfortune.
  • In 0.17, Sure Blade and Enslavement were removed. Darkness was set to level 6 instead of level 7! Gell's Gravitas became level 3 and smite-targeted, but no longer confusing enemies. Silence was now inside Book of Dreams. Metabolic Englaciation was set to level 5.
  • In 0.16, Gell's Gravitas was added.
  • Prior to 0.14, you could find Polymorph in the Book of Transfigurations. It caused an effect similar to a wand of polymorph.
