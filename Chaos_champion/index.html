A half-mortal, half-demonic creature, with its hellish heritage asserting itself through a certain devilish cast to its features.


It possesses chaotic, reality-warping powers.

Useful Info

Chaos champions are demonspawn who can smite you with chaos beams and cast Random Bolts. Their spell Call of Chaos applies (usually) positive status effects to nearby allies. They can be found in the Pandemonium.

  • Chaos champions were removed in 0.19.
