Forceful Invitation

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article is up to date for the latest stable release of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.
Summons some creatures from one of the Snake Pit, the Spider Nest, the Swamp, the Shoals, the Elven Halls, the Vaults, or the Crypt; the specific branch varies with the caster.

Forceful Invitation is a monster-only spell which summons 1d3 monsters from one of the following randomly selected branches, with a given branch being equally likely to be fixed per-caster. Its summon cap is 3.

Branch Monster, chance in branch cast[1]
The Snake Pit S Black mamba 57.14% N Naga warrior 28.57% S Mana viper 14.29%
The Spider's Nest s Wolf spider 50.00% s Tarantella 37.50% s Orb spider 12.50%
The Swamp n Bog body 57.14% k Swamp drake 28.57% F Goliath frog 14.29%
The Shoals t Snapping turtle 50.00% H Manticore 33.33% m Merfolk aquamancer 16.67%
The Elven Halls e Deep elf pyromancer 35.71% e Deep elf zephyrmancer 35.71% e Deep elf knight 28.57%
The Vaults u Very ugly thing 50.00% O Ironbound thunderhulk 33.33% p Vault sentinel 16.67%
The Crypt W Freezing wraith 50.00% z Skeletal warrior 37.50% p Necromancer 12.50%

The following enemies cast Forceful Invitation:

  • x Worldbinder

History

  • Prior to 0.28, Forceful Invitation could summon creatures from the Lair and Orcish Mines. Its monster assortment was also less dangerous.
  • The monster list was altered in 0.19.
  • Forceful Invitation was introduced in 0.14.
