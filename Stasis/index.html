Stasis

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article is up to date for the latest stable release of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.

The stasis intrinsic, exclusive to Formicids, provides immunity from a wide variety of time and space altering effects:

  • Teleportation
    • except for level shifts in the Abyss
  • Blinking
  • Haste, Slow, Swiftness
  • Paralysis
  • Berserk

Stasis will not block any of the following:

  • Effects that warp space around you: Lugonu's Bend Space, Passage of Golubria
  • "Paralysis" inflicted by the onset of stat zero
  • Transporters (not to be confused with teleport traps)
  • Banishment
  • Other disabling effects: Petrify, Sleep

History

  • Prior to 0.25, stasis did not block Swiftness.
  • In 0.18, the amulet of stasis was removed, the primary and only way for most characters to get this intrinsic.
  • Prior to 0.18, stasis blocked Okawaru's Finesse.
