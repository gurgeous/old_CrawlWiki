 Book of Power
Primary school Conjuration
Rarity 6/20
A book of powerful magic spells, crackling and shuddering with barely-contained energies.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Iskenderun's Battlesphere Charms/Conjuration 5
b - Venom Bolt Conjuration/Poison 5
c - Bolt of Magma Conjuration/Earth/Fire 5
d - Iron Shot Conjuration/Earth 6
e - Orb of Destruction Conjuration 7
f - Spellforged Servitor Conjuration/Summoning 7


History

  • In 0.20, Iskenderun's Mystic Blast and Fulminant Prism were replaced with Iskenderun's Battlesphere.
  • In 0.16 Spellforged Servitor was added.
  • In 0.13 Poisonous Cloud was replaced with Fulminant Prism.
  • Prior to 0.11, this book also contained Mass Confusion.
