A small, powerless larva of a killer bee.

Useful Info

Killer bee larva were immature killer bees, too young and undeveloped to pose any serious threat. However, if they happened to cross a tile containing a honeycomb or royal jelly, they immediately matured into a mature killer bee. They could be found most often in killer bee vaults, and were occasionally seen elsewhere as a possible shapeshifter form.

Tips & Tricks

None required.

History

Killer bee larvae were removed from Crawl in 0.11.
