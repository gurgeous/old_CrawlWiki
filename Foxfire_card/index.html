The card depicts a swamp at night, suffused with an eerie glow.

The Foxfire card summons a pack of identical insect monsters. Beware of hostile monsters in the summoned pack.

Power Level 0: 1+1d3 monsters, friendly 75% of the time

Power Level 1: 3+1d3 monsters, mostly friendly

Power Level 2: 6+1d3 monsters, mostly friendly

  • b Butterfly
  • v Insubstantial wisp
  • y Killer bee
  • y Vampire mosquito
  • y Hornet

The Foxfire card could be found in the following decks over the past several versions.
