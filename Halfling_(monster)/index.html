A humanoid of diminutive stature.


Useful Info

Halflings are rather non-threatening creatures that are extremely rare. If you see one, it's probably a shapeshifter or part of a vault.

Tips & Tricks

Like most other monster variants of player races, halflings are mostly harmless unless they manage to find some decent equipment.
