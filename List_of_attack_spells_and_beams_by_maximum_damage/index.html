List of attack spells and beams by maximum damage

From CrawlWiki

Version 0.14: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Assumes no resistance except where specified explicitly.

The damage specified is (approximately) maximum and average damage is half that. Generation is done approximately by throwing the number of dice specified, with the number of faces calculated from maximum damage. Note that there may be small errors in the numbers.

See also Table of attack spells and beams by maximum range.

Warning: this is for player spells only. Monster spells, while possibly having the same name, do different damage. For instance, monster Crystal Spear does not do 23 + power maximum damage, but 48 + power*3/10 = 48 + hit_dice*18/5.
