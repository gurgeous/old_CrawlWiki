Category:Medium monsters

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Medium monsters are a diverse group of creatures with few common traits. Medium humanoids can use all armour and all but the largest weapons, though some species may have further restrictions. They are larger than small monsters and smaller than large monsters.
