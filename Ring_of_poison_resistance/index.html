Name Ring of poison resistance
Icon
This ring provides protection from the effects of poisons and venom.

The ring of poison resistance grants poison resistance when worn.

Desirability

Poison is a threat from the very beginning of the game, and one Lair branch is guaranteed to be poison-themed. This makes poison resistance almost required to get through certain parts of the game, and rings of poison resistance are a conveniently swappable way to get it.
