Useful Info

Storm dragons are enormous, electricity-themed monsters. One of the beefier members of dragonkind, storm dragons are faster, can take more abuse and can deal more melee damage than their fire- and cold-themed cousins, and their breath attack can be particularly devastating if you lack resistance and let them multizap you. Although they can be very dangerous when encountered out of depth, they tend to only generate in the Depths, the Vaults, and the Realm of Zot.

Tips & Tricks

Killing a storm dragon may provide storm dragon scales. This provides electricity resistance and strong defense, though at a high enough evasion penalty to make spellcasting very difficult.
