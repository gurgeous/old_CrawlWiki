Desirability

The crown of Dyrovepreva'[1] combines decent AC, a boost to intelligence, and two occasionally-critical intrinsics (electricity resistance and see invisible) into one handy piece of equipment. Although there are better artefact hats, this is still a better item overall than any non-artefact headgear you can acquire, perhaps barring a hat of willpower. It is definitely worth considering, at the very least.
