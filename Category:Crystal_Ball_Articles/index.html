These articles refer to the upcoming version, at this point in time 0.27. They are gathered here so that, when it is released, they can be quickly updated to reflect the changes, and not retain future-tense sections referring to the present.

When a new version comes out, update the articles in this category, and them remove them from it. Mostly this will just involve a few tense changes.

Remember to remove the "[[Category:Crystal Ball Articles]]" from the bottom of the page when you're done updating an article.
