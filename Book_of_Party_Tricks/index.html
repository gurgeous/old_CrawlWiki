 Book of Party Tricks
A book containing a variety of fun spells guaranteed to liven up any wizarding party, as well as a number of toasts, jokes, and party games.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Apportation Translocation 1
b - Alistair's Intoxication Poison/Transmutation 5


History

  • In 0.26, Summon Butterflies was removed.
  • In 0.19, Lesser Beckoning was added.
  • In 0.16, Blink was replaced by Invisibility.
  • In 0.11, Projected Noise was replaced by Tukima's Dance.
