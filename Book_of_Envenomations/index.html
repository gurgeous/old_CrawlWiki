Obsolete: This article refers to an aspect of the game which has been removed. It is retained for historical reference only.


Type Book
Name Book of Envenomations
A book of magic spells, glowing with a sickly green light. This book was first written by Olgreb, the infamous Venom Mage, who murdered many of his rivals by sending them highly poisonous copies as gifts. Current editions are fortunately harmless.

The book of Envenomations is the more advanced text for Poison Magic.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Spider Form Transmutations/Poison 3
b - Olgreb's Toxic Radiance Poison 4
c - Alistair's Intoxication Transmutations/Poison 5
d - Poisonous Cloud Air/Poison/Conjuration 7

Strategy

While the spells contained are generally quite useful, none of them provides significant assistance against poison-resistant enemies. Most poison casters will need other spells, possibly gifted by Sif Muna or Vehumet.

History

  • Removed in 0.19.
