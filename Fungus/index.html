A lumpy grey fungus, growing well in the dank underground dungeon.

Useful Info

Fungi are harmless, stationary monsters similar to plants. Like plants, they wither and quickly die when damaged by any attack.

Tips & Ticks

  • Prior to 0.28, fungi, as a plant-type monster, had negative energy immunity.
  • Prior to 0.26, fungi did not wither when damaged.
  • Prior to 0.24, followers of Fedhas Madash could evolve fungi into wandering mushrooms.
  • Prior to 0.10 and 0.11, fungi could be used to easily apply skill points or identify weapons.
