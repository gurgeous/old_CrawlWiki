Category:Drown resistance

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

These monsters are capable of being submerged in deep water without drowning. Note that monsters with drown resistance whose habitat is land will not enter water voluntarily - they just won't die if they end up there (e.g. by being confused).
