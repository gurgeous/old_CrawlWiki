Obsolete: This article refers to an aspect of the game which has been removed. It is retained for historical reference only.


Type Book
Name Akashic Record
The last and greatest work of the Warper Golubria, detailing the most powerful Translocations spells in existence. It is notoriously difficult to track down and understand, as copies of the book and their contents have a tendency to shift between planes at a moment's notice.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Dispersal Translocation 6
b - Malign Gateway Summoning/Translocation 7
c - Disjunction Translocation 8
d - Controlled Blink Translocation 8


History

Removed in 0.19.

Prior to 0.17, this book contained Singularity.

This book was added in 0.16.
