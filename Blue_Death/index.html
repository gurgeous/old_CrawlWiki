Useful Info

A Blue Death is a tier-1 demon which attacks with electricity and cold damage spells, as well as extremely random summoned creatures.

Spells: Lightning Bolt (3d18), Bolt of Cold (3d21), Call Imp, Shadow Creatures, and Teleport Other

Tips & Tricks

  • The Shadow Creatures spell creates a random area-appropriate monster or band of monsters. Depending on where you are fighting, this can result in nearly any enemy being summoned up to fight you. Having Abjuration ready may help.
  • Blue Deaths are vulnerable to fire and have unimpressive EV. Any character can wand of fire one easily.

History

Blue deaths were removed in 0.10.
