A demon lapdog of the queen of Tartarus, bound to hunt her enemies. Though it may appear alone, it's unlikely to remain so for long — it has the terrible power to set creatures of nightmare on its victim's trails, though it can only ever use this power exactly once.

Useful Info

Doom hounds are demonic canines that roam the realm of Tartarus and under the companionship of Grunn. Each doom hound can howl once, which gives you the Howl status condition. For the next several turns, other denizens of Tartarus (such as bone dragons, shadow dragons, tormentors, or even Tzitzimimeh) will be called in to near your location. These reinforcements are not summons, immune to abjuration and worth XP and piety.

Tips & Tricks

  • They have very low MR, especially for the Hells. A simple wand of paralysis will shut them down long enough to smash them.
  • If you can't kill them before they get a howl off, try to retreat to an area where you can duck out of sight of any particularly nasty summons.
  • A potion of cancellation will remove the Howl status, if you're in a pinch.

History

  • Doom hounds were introduced in 0.18.
