You are the player or adventurer and Dungeon Crawl is a one-player game. Player ghosts can provide a kind of a limited interaction with other (former) players.

Your character or hero is represented by the @ symbol. There are also other characters represented by an @, such as Sigmund and Donald. The player has hit points, MP (to cast spells), attributes, and experience.

Starting stats

When you pick a character, the game determines its stats this way:

  1. Take the race base value for each stat (see table below)
  2. Add class modifiers (see table below)
  3. Give a pool of 8 potential points, or 15 for Demigods and Demonspawns
  4. Use those points to raise every stat to at least 1
  5. For each remaining point, choose randomly a stat. If this stat is under 18, or over 17 with a 50% chance, this stat will be increased by 1. If the stat is increased, one point is substracted from the pool.
