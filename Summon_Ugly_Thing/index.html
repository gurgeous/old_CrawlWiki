 Summon Ugly Thing
Level 6
School1 Summoning
Source(s)
  • Book of Summonings
Casting noise 5
Spell noise 0
This spell summons a mutated otherworldly creature to the caster's aid.
You can sustain at most three creatures summoned by this spell.

Summon Ugly Thing is a level 6 Summonings spell which summons a single friendly ugly thing (or a very ugly thing 80% of the time at max spell power). The summoned things last a long time and some of their branded attacks can be quite powerful, especially against vulnerable enemies.

History

Summon Ugly Thing was removed in 0.14.

Prior to 0.10, this spell could occasionally summon hostile ugly things, particularly at low spell power.

In 0.6, the spell was improved so that it always summoned very ugly things at high spell power. It was weakened to its current form in 0.7.
