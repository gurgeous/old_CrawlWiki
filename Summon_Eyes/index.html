Summon Eyeballs

Summon Eyeballs is a monster-only spell which summons several of the following eyeball-type monsters into existence:

  • G Floating eye (32%)
  • G Eye of draining (26%)
  • G Golden eye (19%)
  • G Shining eye (13%)
  • G Great orb of eyes (6%)
  • G Eye of devastation (3%)

This spell is especially harmful as a player simply cannot easily protect against the wide variety of unique, unpleasant abilities these monsters possess all at once, and should kill the summoner as quickly as possible to unsummon these enemies all at once.

The following enemies cast Summon Eyeballs:

  • J Dissolution
  • & Mnoleg

The following enemies may be able to cast Summon Eyeballs, depending on their spell set:

  • & Pandemonium lord (4.2% chance)
