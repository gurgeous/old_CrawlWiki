Ghostly Sacrifice

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.25: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.
Uses an ally's motivating essence to create a blast of negative energy, draining any living creature it engulfs. The being used to fuel the spell, no matter whether living or construct, is consumed and unmade in the process.

Ghostly Sacrifice is a monster-only spell that destroys one creature allied with the caster (summoned creatures may also be used as fuel), creating an explosion of draining negative energy in the process. It uses smite targeting and deals significant damage to creatures susceptible to negative energy, so monsters that can cast this spell should be a high priority.

The following enemies cast Ghostly Sacrifice:

  • L Halazid warlock (5d22 damage)
