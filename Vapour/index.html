A cloud of weird vapour, only visible to those with supernaturally enhanced eyesight. As it floats aimlessly through the dungeon, it builds up an electric charge, which it will occasionally discharge in your direction.

Useful Info

Vapours are essentially pockets of very bad weather. They are naturally invisible and permanently confused, spending most of their time moving randomly. Every so often, however, they'll fire off a bolt of lightning, dealing electricity damage to anything that happens to be in its way. Due to their invisibility, a random lightning bolt out of a seemingly clear space is the first clue you'll have that they're around. They are normally only found in the Swamp, the Cloud Mages' Chambers, or certain vaults, but may be summoned by drawing the Foxfire card.
