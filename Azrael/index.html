Useful Info

Azrael the Boundless Flame is a unique efreet, often accompanied by several hell hounds and fire elementals. Like all efreets, he comes armed with a flaming scimitar, but he's much happier hitting you with his wide variety of Fire Magic and damnation spells while his pets do their work.

Tips & Tricks

  • As a demon, he is immune to silence. Fire resistance will defend against most of his spells and melee attacks, but nothing will protect you from his damnation. Also, his allies are all immune to fire, so don't think he'll just wait patiently in the back row to throw his fire spells.
  • If you've got good stabbing capabilities, take advantage of the fact that he can't see invisible.
  • He is vulnerable to cold, so Ice Magic or a wand of iceblast can deal decent damage quickly; this also has the added benefit of tearing through his allies in short order.
