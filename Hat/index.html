Name Hat
Size to wear Any except giant
Armour Class 0
Encumbrance rating +0
Maximum Enchantment +2
Grants nothing
Time to Wear 5


A cloth hat.

Hats are a form of head armour. Unlike helmets, they provide no base AC, but can be used by characters with beaks and all but the largest of horns and antennae. They can be maximally enchanted to +2.

Hats can have the following egos:

  • Intelligence (+3)
  • Willpower (+40)
  • Stealth (+50)
  • See invisible

History

  • Prior to 0.27, hats couldn't have Stealth ego.
  • Prior to 0.20, hats could have Spirit shield ego.
  • Prior to 0.19, you could find hats with cold and fire resistance egos in ice caves and volcanoes respectively.
  • Hats were added in 0.14, consolidating wizard hats and caps.
