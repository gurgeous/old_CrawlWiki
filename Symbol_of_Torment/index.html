Symbol of Torment

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.26: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.
Calls on the powers of darkness to cause agonising injury to any living thing in the caster's vicinity, cutting their resilience in half. This damage is never directly lethal.

Symbol of Torment is a monsters-only spell that calls on the powers of darkness to cause agonising torment to everything in sight. Unlike Agony, it ignores willpower and halves the health of all creatures in sight that do not possess torment immunity (though negative energy resistance can somewhat mitigate the damage taken from torment).

While the spell technically affects all monsters in sight, all monsters that cast it are torment-immune, and it is most commonly seen in areas such as the Tomb or Hell, where nearly all of the inhabitants are also torment-immune.

There are a few ways for a player to duplicate this effect, although beware that doing so will target every living thing in your line of sight (including you, if you're alive). For a list of methods and ways to minimize the costs, see torment.

The following enemies cast Symbol of Torment:

  • 1 Brimstone Fiend
  • z Curse skull
  • z Curse toe
  • & Ereshkigal
  • & Gloorx Vloq
  • 1 Ice Fiend
  • M Khufu
  • M Menkaure
  • M Mummy priest
  • z Murray
  • M Royal mummy
  • 2 Tormentor
  • 1 Tzitzimitl

The following enemies may be able to cast Symbol of Torment, depending on their spell set:

  • & Pandemonium lord (3.4% chance)

History

Prior to 0.8, players could cast a version of Symbol of Torment as a level 6 Necromancy spell. Unlike the monster version, this spell would not function if the caster had torment immunity.
