Amulet of magic regeneration

Type Jewellery
Name Amulet of magic regeneration
Icon
An amulet that substantially increases the wearer's normal rate of magic regeneration. In order to function, it must first attune itself to the wearer's body with full reserves of magic.

An amulet of magic regeneration increases its wearer's MP regeneration rate by a flat 0.2 MP/turn, greatly hastening the MP recovery rate of your character. This benefit becomes less pronounced as your base MP regeneration rate rises (as a result of having more maximum MP), but its effect is always substantial. The increased MP recovery does not take effect until the character's MP has reached maximum once while wearing it.

Desirability

An amulet of magic regeneration is quite welcome for spellcasters, especially ones who don't have access to some type of channeling or Vehumet's MP recovery. Characters with spirit shield by some means other than an amulet of guardian spirit may also find it useful.

History

The amulet of magic regeneration was added in 0.18. This amulet was useless for followers of Pakellas, who don't regenerate MP.
