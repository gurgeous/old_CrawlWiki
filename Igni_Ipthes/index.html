Racial Restrictions

Demigods cannot worship Igni Ipthes (or any other god).

  • Using enchanted weapons and armor

Given Abilities

Piety level (0):

  • Enchant weapon scrolls no longer have a chance of failure.

Piety level (*):

  • Increases AC of metal body armour. Allows any size character to wear metal body armour. Reduces the effects of the Deformed mutation.

Piety level (**):

  • Allows you to add the flame brand to a weapon. Monsters wielding a flaming weapon will get burned and drop it.

Piety level (***):

  • Divine Bellows: pushes enemies away from you.

Piety level (****):

  • Fire Fortress: surround yourself with flame clouds.

Piety level (*****):

  • Turn a weapon into an artefact.
