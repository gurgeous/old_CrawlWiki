This spell violently unbinds the magic which keeps the caster's summoned creatures in this world, engulfing anything near them in a maelstrom of magical force. The more powerful the creature released, the stronger the resulting backlash will be.


Forceful Dismissal is a level 6 Summonings spell which immediately dismisses all of your current summoned creatures. In the process, all monsters standing adjacent to them take irresistible, unavoidable damage. Damage formula is (1+ summon HD/4)d(7 + spellpower/8).

History

Forceful Dismissal was removed in 0.16.

Forceful Dismissal was added in 0.14.
