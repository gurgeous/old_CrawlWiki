Summon Greater Demon

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.27: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.
Calls forth one of the greater demons of Pandemonium to serve the caster. With high power, the summoned demon will be less likely to be initially hostile — but the strongest demons may eventually turn against their summoner regardless.

Summon Greater Demon is a monster-only Summonings spell which calls a single greater demon (i.e. tier 1 or 2) into existence.

The following enemies cast Summon Greater Demon:

  • & Cerebov
  • e Deep elf demonologist
  • & Dispater
  • L Dread lich
  • & Ereshkigal

The following enemies may be able to cast Summon Greater Demon, depending on their spell set:

  • & Pandemonium lord (8.4% chance)

  • Prior to 0.27, this spell was a Level 7 Summonings spell available to players.
  • Prior to 0.10, this spell only summoned Tier 1 demons. However, at the time the category included many demons that are now classified as Tier 2, such as balrugs and cacodemons. This meant that every summon would eventually turn hostile.
