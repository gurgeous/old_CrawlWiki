 Disjunction
Level 8
School1 Translocation
Source(s) Book of the Warp
Casting noise 6
Spell noise 0
Power Cap 200
Flags Escape, Utility
Destabilizes the space in a sphere around the caster for a while, causing anyone nearby to blink away from the caster. The chance of blinking depends on the distance from the caster, being nearly certain when directly adjacent. It causes magical contamination when cast.

Disjunction is a level 8 Translocation spell which makes you emit an aura that might blink away close-by monsters.

Effect

When cast an aura of disjunction with radius of 4 appears. The duration of the aura is randomized and depends on the spell power, minimum duration is 6 turns.

The chance of monster blinking away depends on how close it is to the centre. Magic resistance isn't effective against Disjunction, if the monster is able to blink it can be blinked away.

Each time you cast the spell, you'll get from 0.75 to 1.25 points of magic contamination.[1]

History

Disjunction was added in 0.12
