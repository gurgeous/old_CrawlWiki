Digs a tunnel through unworked rock.

Dig is a monster-only spell which instantly carves away all rock walls, slimy walls, or iron grates in a straight line from the caster. It has no effect on stone, metal, green crystal, or any other kind of wall. This spell does not harm you in any way, but makes both escape and limiting the number of enemies you face at once difficult.

Player characters may duplicate this spell's effects with a wand of digging.

The following enemies cast Dig:

  • 2 Cacodemon
  • T Deep troll earth mage
  • g Jorgrun
  • & Mnoleg
