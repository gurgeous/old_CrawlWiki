 Book of Annihilations
Primary school Conjuration
A tome of arcane fury, greatly sought after by sorcerers. It allows the gifted and resourceful to conjure destruction with ease.

The Book of Annihilations contains the most powerful Conjuration spells available.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Chain Lightning Air/Conjuration 9
b - Fire Storm Conjuration/Fire 9
c - Shatter Earth 9


History

  • In 0.28, Shatter was moved from the Book of Earth into this book.
  • In 0.27, the book lost Lehudib's Crystal Spear and Absolute Zero.
  • In 0.25, the spell Glaciate got replaced by Absolute Zero.
  • Prior to 0.17, reading the book of Annihilations required you to have both level 6 in Spellcasting and level 10 in Conjurations.
  • Prior to 0.14, the Book of Annihilations contained Orb of Destruction.
  • Prior to 0.12, failing to memorise a spell from this book could produce severe Conjurations miscast effects. The Book of Annihilations was one of the books gifted by Vehumet. The followers of this god were able to read this book without the required amounts of Spellcasting and Conjurations.
