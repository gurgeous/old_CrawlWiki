Fires a penetrating bolt of negative energy which drains any living creature it strikes.

Bolt of Draining is a monster-only spell which fires a bolt of negative energy, dealing damage and draining any creatures in its path. Negative energy resistance reduces the damage dealt by this spell by 50% (rN+), 75% (rN++), or 100% (rN+++). Many monsters are capable of casting Bolt of Draining with extremely irritating results: a loss of skill levels which only disappears after you've gained sufficient experience.

The following enemies cast Bolt of Draining:

  • 6 Black sun (3d22 damage)
  • e Deep elf death mage (3d22 damage)
  • W Eidolon (3d21 damage)
  • L Halazid warlock (3d27 damage)
  • o Nergalle (3d18 damage)
  • o Orc sorcerer (3d17 damage)
  • D Serpent of Hell (Tartarus) (3d24 damage)
  • D Shadow dragon (3d24 damage)
  • 1 Tzitzimitl (3d29 damage)
  • V Vampire mage (3d18 damage)

The following enemies may be able to cast Bolt of Draining, depending on their spell set:

  • z Ancient champion (3d21 damage, 50.0% chance)
  • L Ancient lich (3d33 damage, 20.0% chance)
  • e Deep elf mage (3d17 damage, 16.7% chance)
  • L Lich (3d27 damage, 20.0% chance)
  • & Pandemonium lord (5.2% chance)
