Slows the actions of a creature.

Slow is a status effect and a level 1 Hexes spell that makes the target slower.

Effect

Slowed creatures take 50% longer for movement and all other actions. If you also are hasted, the two effects will cancel each other out. This can seriously reduce the damage output of affected creatures, and it is much easier to escape slowed pursurers (but much harder to escape when slowed).

Formicids are completely immune to the effect due to their stasis.

Spell

If the target fails a willpower check, they will be slowed. At higher spell power, the target is slowed for a longer period of time.

The Arcane Marksman background starts with this spell memorized, and Hedge Wizards may learn it from turn one.

Sources

Other than the spell, a piece from Xom's chessboard, clouds of miasma, curare-tipped darts, Metabolic Englaciation, or Leda's Liquefaction (on monsters) will all have the same "slow" effect, but some of them will check other parameters or resistances instead of willpower.

Monster Version

The monster version of this spell functions similarly to the player version.

The following enemies may be able to cast Slow, depending on their spell set:

  • & Pandemonium lord (3.4% chance)

History

  • Prior to 0.28, the Slow spell was a level 2 Hexes spell, with a power cap of 200.
  • Prior to 0.18, an amulet of stasis could protect you from Slow.
  • In 0.17, Slow was removed from the Book of Hinderance.
  • In 0.10, the spell received a small power boost.
  • Prior to 0.8, slowing made every action take twice as long.
