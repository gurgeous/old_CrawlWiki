Red Sonja is the first module for Dungeon Sprint. It places you in a maze of small, interconnected chambers filled with gold, shops, and a wide variety of unique opponents. Remembering what one can expect in each room is vital, as certain side paths are deadly to unprepared characters.

See the Sprint guide for further advice.
