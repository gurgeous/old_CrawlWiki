Fountain

Version 0.25: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Fountains are a Dungeon feature often found in many vaults. They are almost entirely decorative, but water-filled fountains act as water tiles for the purposes of allowing aquatic creatures to swim through them, extinguishing sticky flames, and generating steam and freezing vapour when fire and ice spells are cast over them.

List of Fountains

Fountain of clear blue water

A decorative fountain, flowing with clear water.

Sparkling fountain

A decorative fountain, flowing with brightly sparkling water.

Fountain of blood

A decorative fountain, spurting with foul-looking blood.

Dry fountain

A dried-up fountain that has seen better days.

History

  • Prior to 0.15, mimics could disguise themselves as fountains.
  • Prior to 0.14, players could quaff from fountains for various effects.
  • Prior to 0.12, fountains dried by the player would eventually flow again given enough time (1% chance every 20 turns), becoming whatever kind of fountain they were beforehand.
