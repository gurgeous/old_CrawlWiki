Obsolete: This article refers to an aspect of the game which has been removed. It is retained for historical reference only.

The Bones card summons a skeletal monster to your immediate vicinity.

  • Power Level 0: Z Large skeleton, friendly 75% of the time
  • Power Level 1: z skeletal warrior, friendly 83% of the time
  • Power Level 2: D bone dragon, friendly 87% of the time
