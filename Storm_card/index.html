The card depicts a ship at sea sinking in a thunderstorm.

Drawing this card unleashes a storm of wind and electrical discharges. Increasing card power will result in a stronger wind force and stronger more numerous discharges.

The Storm card attempts to blow enemies away, then creates a surge of electrical explosions (similar to Qazlal) that cause significant damage to enemies. The storm will never harm the player.

History

  • Prior to 0.23, the Storm card would form ring of thunder clouds about 3 tiles thick 2 tiles away from the player, and summon air elementals.
  • This card was one of 23 cards to be kept in the deck simplification of 0.19.
  • Prior to 0.17, the card had a 50% chance to summon a twister.
  • The Storm card was added in 0.15 to replace the Spark card.
