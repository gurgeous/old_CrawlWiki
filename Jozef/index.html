Useful Info

Jozef was a fairly ordinary human unique, often generating with a moderately powerful melee weapon and an assortment of annoying spells, and occasionally with throwing nets or a sling. He could be found in the Lair or the Elven Halls.

Spells: Teleport Other, Healing, Venom Bolt, Slow.

Tips & Tricks

  • While not particularly lethal in his own right Jozef can be incredibly annoying. He has a habit of slowing and poisoning you, then teleporting you into some exciting new death trap. Try to engage him on a cleared level where others won't interfere.

History

Jozef was removed in 0.11.
