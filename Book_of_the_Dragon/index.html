 Book of the Dragon
A magical book of spells which allow some command over dragons and their aspects.

Spells

Tile Spell Type Level
a - Cause Fear Hexes 4
b - Dragon Form Transmutation 7
c - Dragon's Call Summoning 9


History

  • In 0.17, Flight was replaced by Flame Tongue.
  • In 0.14, Summon Dragon was replaced by Dragon's Call.
