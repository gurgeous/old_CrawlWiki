Ice Magic

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.21: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Ice Magic skill determines how easily you can cast spells that employ ice, cold, and frost.

Ice Magic offers a balance between offensive and defensive spells. Ice-based spells are generally resistible with cold resistance.
