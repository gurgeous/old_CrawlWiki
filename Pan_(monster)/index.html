Useful Info

Pan was a unique satyr who was never included in a stable release of Crawl. He was intended to roam the Enchanted Forest with a band of satyrs and fauns, disabling you with powerful Hexes while his allies riddled you with arrows and sling bullets.

History

Pan was a unique that existed in trunk for a very brief period of time. He was originally intended to be included in 0.13, but was disabled shortly after his introduction, and removed not long thereafter.
